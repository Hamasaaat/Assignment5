* Women In Tech Summit

Women in Tech® Global, the world’s leading organization supporting women in STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics).

* GITEX 2018

by Rania A.

15th October 2018

The major tech event in Dubai. An extraordinary exhibition dedicated to technology.

GITEX (Gulf Information Technology Exhibition) is one of the largest and most influential technology exhibitions in the Middle East,
held annually in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. It serves as a platform for global tech companies to showcase their latest innovations,
products, and services. The event attracts a diverse audience, including tech professionals, businesses, government officials,
and tech enthusiasts from around the world.

Key Features of GITEX Dubai:

  1. Technology Showcase:
    The event features the latest advancements in areas like AI, cybersecurity, cloud computing, blockchain, 5G, robotics, and smart cities.
  2. Exhibitions & Demonstrations:
    Major technology companies, startups, and innovators display their cutting-edge products and solutions.
  3. Networking & Partnerships:
    It provides opportunities for businesses to connect, collaborate, and form partnerships, while also offering a space for discussions and workshops.
  4. Conferences & Workshops:
    GITEX hosts numerous expert-led sessions, seminars, and keynote speeches focused on the future of technology and digital transformation
